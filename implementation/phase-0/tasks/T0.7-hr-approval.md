# T0.7 — HR/People Approval

**Phase:** 0  
**Owner:** HR or People Lead  
**Estimate:** 3 days  
**Depends on:** None

## Objective

Obtain written HR/People approval for the "no individual performance use" policy.

## What to present to HR

```text
Prepare a 1-page brief for HR/People Lead:

1. WHAT THE PLATFORM DOES:
   - Collects PR metadata (not code, not prompts)
   - Computes team-level metrics (not individual scores)
   - Shows a dashboard for team leads and managers
   - Helps teams understand whether AI is creating delivery value

2. WHAT THE PLATFORM DOES NOT DO:
   - No individual rankings or leaderboards
   - No "who uses AI most" views
   - No personal dashboards
   - No performance reviews or promotions
   - No surveillance of individual developers

3. THE POLICY:
   - "No individual performance use" is a hard rule in the platform
   - If a manager attempts to use metrics for individual scoring:
     1. Pause expansion for that team
     2. Notify Platform Lead and HR/People Lead
     3. Re-brief the manager and team
     4. Remove individual-level exports from that manager's access
     5. Resume only after misuse risk is addressed

4. SAFETY MECHANISMS:
   - Psychological safety pulse survey (6 questions, anonymous)
   - If score drops below 3.5: pause metric expansion
   - Developers can challenge metric interpretation
   - Blameless retrospectives for any issues

5. BENEFIT:
   - Teams can adopt AI safely without creating surveillance anxiety
   - Leadership gets team-level visibility without individual monitoring
   - The platform protects developers from misuse by design
```

## What HR should confirm

```text
- [ ] HR/People Lead understands the platform's purpose and data usage.
- [ ] Written acknowledgement of "no individual performance use" policy.
- [ ] HR/People Lead agrees the platform does not constitute individual surveillance.
- [ ] Misuse escalation procedure is acceptable.
- [ ] Any HR concerns or requirements documented.
```

## Acceptance criteria

- [ ] HR/People Lead has reviewed the platform's purpose and data usage.
- [ ] Written acknowledgement of "no individual performance use" policy received.
- [ ] HR/People Lead agrees the platform does not constitute individual surveillance.
- [ ] Misuse escalation procedure is acceptable.
- [ ] Approval documented in [Phase0]_exit_report.pdf.
