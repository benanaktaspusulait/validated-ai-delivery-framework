## Feasibility checklist

- [ ] Data is available and accessible.
- [ ] Problem is solvable with ML (vs. rules or process change).
- [ ] Minimum viable model complexity identified.
- [ ] Regulatory classification determined.
- [ ] Budget and timeline agreed.
- [ ] Ethical risks assessed.
