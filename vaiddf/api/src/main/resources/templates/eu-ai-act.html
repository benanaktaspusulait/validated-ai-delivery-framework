EU AI Act Compliance Mapping

How VAIDDF addresses EU AI Act requirements

Fully Implemented: 6 Partially Implemented: 3 Planned: 2
Article 9 — Risk Management

Risk Management System

Requirement: Establish, implement, and maintain a risk management system.

VAIDDF: Fully Implemented

Policy engine with OPA/Rego rules. Risk scoring in drift detection. Automated guardrails in Phase 4.

Article 10 — Data Governance

Data Governance and Data Quality

Requirement: Training, validation, and testing datasets meet quality criteria.

VAIDDF: Fully Implemented

Drift detection monitors data quality. PSI scoring for reference vs production data. Feature-level analysis.

Article 11 — Technical Documentation

Technical Documentation

Requirement: Keep technical documentation up to date.

VAIDDF: Fully Implemented

Model registry stores metadata. Governance annotations document decisions. OpenAPI spec auto-generated.

Article 12 — Record-Keeping

Automatic Logging

Requirement: Automatic recording of events (logs) over the lifetime of the system.

VAIDDF: Fully Implemented

Immutable audit log. Every model creation, deployment, and policy evaluation logged. No deletion possible.

Article 13 — Transparency

Information to Deployers

Requirement: Provide deployers with sufficient information for use.

VAIDDF: Fully Implemented

Dashboard shows model status. API documentation via OpenAPI. CLI provides quick access.

Article 14 — Human Oversight

Human Oversight Measures

Requirement: Enable effective oversight by humans.

VAIDDF: Partially Implemented

Model deployment requires approval. Rollback capability. Phase 4 adds automated enforcement with human override.

Article 15 — Accuracy, Robustness, Cybersecurity

Accuracy and Robustness

Requirement: Designed for appropriate levels of accuracy and robustness.

VAIDDF: Partially Implemented

Drift detection monitors accuracy degradation. Fairness checking ensures robustness across groups.

Article 26 — Deployer Obligations

Obligations of Deployers

Requirement: Deployers must use AI systems according to instructions.

VAIDDF: Fully Implemented

Governance annotations enforce deployment rules. Policy engine blocks non-compliant models.

Article 47 — Post-Market Monitoring

Post-Market Monitoring System

Requirement: Establish a post-market monitoring system.

VAIDDF: Fully Implemented

Continuous drift monitoring. Automated alerts. Model lifecycle tracking from registration to deprecation.

Article 50 — Transparency for Certain AI Systems

Transparency Measures

Requirement: Inform natural persons that they are interacting with an AI system.

VAIDDF: Partially Implemented

Model metadata includes AI system information. UI shows model status. Full transparency dashboard planned.
