# AI Delivery Platform: Quick Start Guide

## 1. Introduction
Welcome to the AI Delivery Control Plane. This platform helps you measure and govern AI-assisted software delivery while maintaining quality and psychological safety.

## 2. Prerequisites
- Access to the internal AI Delivery Platform (SSO).
- Admin access to your team's GitHub repositories and Jira projects (for the initial setup).

## 3. Onboarding Steps

### Step 1: Access the Platform
Log in to `https://ai-delivery.internal.example.com` using your corporate SSO credentials.

### Step 2: Configure Your Team
Create a `team-config.yaml` for your team. Use the [Example Configuration](phase-5-enterprise-rollout.md#self-service-team-configuration) as a template.

### Step 3: Map Repositories and Projects
- In the platform UI, go to **Settings > Integrations**.
- Ensure the GitHub App is installed for your organisation.
- Connect your Jira project via the provided API credentials.
- Upload your `team-config.yaml`.

### Step 4: Observation Mode
Your team will start in **Observation Mode**. Data collection begins immediately, but no warnings or blocks will be active. Use this time (typically 2-4 weeks) to calibrate your baseline metrics.

## 4. Understanding the Dashboard
- **AI-assisted PR Rate:** Percentage of PRs using AI.
- **AI Review Debt:** Additional review load generated by AI output.
- **Net AI Delivery Value:** Estimated economic impact after accounting for validation costs.
- **Data Confidence Score:** Reliability of the displayed metrics.
