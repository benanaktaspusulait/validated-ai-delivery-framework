# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

This commitment is consistent with the framework's own thesis: the work succeeds only in an environment of psychological safety, where people can ask questions, disagree and admit uncertainty without fear.

## Expected behaviour

```text
Be respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences.
Give and accept constructive feedback gracefully.
Focus on what is best for the community and the documentation.
Show empathy towards other community members.
Critique ideas and documents, not people.
```

## Unacceptable behaviour

```text
Harassment, intimidation or discrimination in any form.
Personal or political attacks.
Publishing others' private information without explicit permission.
Sustained disruption of discussions or reviews.
Any conduct that would reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting.
```

## Scope

This Code applies within all project spaces (issues, pull requests, discussions) and in public spaces when an individual is representing the project.

## Enforcement

```text
Reports can be sent privately to the maintainers at ai-delivery-framework@example.com or via the repository's private security advisory.
All reports are reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.
Maintainers will respect the privacy and security of the reporter.
```

Maintainers who do not follow or enforce this Code in good faith may face temporary or permanent repercussions as determined by other members of the project's leadership.

## Enforcement actions

| Severity | Example | Response |
|---|---|---|
| Minor | A single inappropriate comment | Private warning and clarification of expectations |
| Moderate | Repeated inappropriate behaviour | Temporary ban from interactions |
| Severe | Harassment or threats | Permanent ban from the project |
